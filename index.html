Introducing:
Mr. and Mrs. Edgar

Hey you, we’re married!!
Consider this the engagement party, save the date, invitation, RSVP, bachelor/ette weekend, rehearsal dinn….. get the picture?
It was supposed to go out the night before our ceremony, but spending quality time with our families was too valuable to pass up!

Regardless, we hope that you can see the love of God through our lives. It's kind of crazy, it isn't always convenient, and it definitely doesn't always make sense, but this love has changed both of our lives forever. When we think about marriage being a picture of God’s selfless sacrifice, we are humbled, challenged, and excited all at the same time.
Still, we dare to hope that this love will continue to be the defining factor of our marriage.

Like we were challenged to think beyond a dress, ring, or table settings from pinterest, we hope that you too are encouraged to think about how you may build your life around the things that are everlasting: loving God and loving people.

May your life be filled with purpose, abundant joy, and peace that is beyond circumstance.
